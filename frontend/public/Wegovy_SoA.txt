TRIAL: Wegovy Semaglutide STEP-1 (NCT03548935)
DOCUMENT: Schedule of Assessments (SoA)

CALENDAR OF CLINIC VISITS:
1.  Screening (Week -2): Labs, ECG, Medical History, BMI check. (3 hours)
2.  Randomization (Week 0): Baseline Weight, First Injection, Lifestyle Counseling. (2 hours)
3.  Week 4: Dose Escalation (0.25mg -> 0.5mg), Weight, AE Screen. (1 hour)
4.  Week 8: Dose Escalation (0.5mg -> 1.0mg), Weight, Counseling. (1 hour)
5.  Week 12: Dose Escalation (1.0mg -> 1.7mg), Weight, AE Screen. (1 hour)
6.  Week 16: Final Escalation (1.7mg -> 2.4mg), Weight, Counseling. (1 hour)
7.  Weeks 20, 28, 36, 44, 52, 60: Maintenance phase checks. Bi-monthly clinic visits. (1 hour each)
8.  Week 68: End-of-treatment assessment. Full labs, weight, ECG. (3 hours)
9.  Week 75: Follow-up visit (7 weeks after last dose). Safety check. (1 hour)

AT-HOME REQUIREMENTS:
- Weekly Subcutaneous Injection: Every Sunday morning.
- Daily Food/Activity Diary: Logged via mobile app.
- Weekly Weight Check: Home scale (Bluetooth synced).

LOGISTICAL BURDEN SCORES:
- Geographic: High (Requires 15 clinic visits over 68 weeks).
- Physical: Moderate (Weekly self-injection).
- Cognitive: Moderate (Daily diary adherence).
- OVERALL SEVERITY: Significant for patients without reliable transportation or with needle phobia.
