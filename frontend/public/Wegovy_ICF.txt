TRIAL: Wegovy Semaglutide STEP-1 (NCT03548935)
DOCUMENT: Informed Consent Form (ICF)

CORE PROMISE (PATIENT MOTIVATION):
Participants are promised access to a highly effective weight-management medication (Semaglutide 2.4mg) that has shown up to 15-20% weight loss in preliminary clinical settings. This "hope of weight loss" is the primary driver for trial retention.

DISCLOSED RISKS (ATTRITION TRIGGERS):
1.  Gastrointestinal Side Effects: You are very likely (up to 70% chance) to experience nausea, diarrhea, or constipation, especially during the first 16 weeks of dose increases.
2.  Pancreatitis: A rare but serious inflammation of the pancreas has been reported.
3.  Gallstones: Rapid weight loss can lead to gallbladder issues.
4.  Thyroid Tumors: In animal studies, similar drugs caused thyroid tumors; the risk in humans is unknown.

COMMITMENT REQUIRED:
- You must commit to 15 in-person visits over 1.5 years.
- You must self-inject weekly.
- You must follow a reduced-calorie diet and increased physical activity plan.

TERMINATION CRITERIA:
You may leave the trial at any time for any reason. If side effects become intolerable (e.g., persistent vomiting), the study doctor may stop your treatment.
