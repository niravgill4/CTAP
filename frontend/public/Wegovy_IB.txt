TRIAL: Wegovy Semaglutide STEP-1 (NCT03548935)
DOCUMENT: Investigator's Brochure (IB)

ADVERSE EVENT INCIDENCE (Phase 3 STEP 1 DATA):
The following systemic friction events were recorded in the STEP 1 trial (n=1961). The simulation engine should prioritize these probabilities during the Titration Phase (Weeks 0-16).

GI ADVERSE EVENTS (SEMAGLUTIDE VS PLACEBO):
- Nausea: 44.2% vs 17.4%
- Diarrhea: 31.5% vs 15.9%
- Vomiting: 24.8% vs 6.5%
- Constipation: 23.4% vs 9.5%
- Abdominal Pain: 14.5% vs 8.1%

SERIOUS ADVERSE EVENTS:
- Gallbladder-related disorders: 2.6% vs 1.2%
- Acute Pancreatitis: 0.2% vs 0.2% (No excess risk vs placebo, but mandatory dropout if occurs).

TEMPORAL DYNAMICS:
GI events are most frequent during dose escalation. Median duration of nausea is 5-8 days. Dropout rate specifically due to AEs in STEP 1 was 7.0% for semaglutide vs 3.1% for placebo.

ATTRITION CALIBRATION:
Total attrition in STEP 1 was ~15%. The majority of dropouts occurred in the first 20 weeks. Demographic mapping indicates higher sensitivity in patients with history of dyspepsia.
